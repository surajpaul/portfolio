What we do

We transform ideas into digital outcomes to elevate the connection between our clients and their customers.

01.Digital Products.

Digital products are the apps, tools, sites, systems —the list is long— that you use to watch, buy, create, control and manage stuff digitally. Through thorough research and after looking into every nook and cranny, we create functional, intuitive, engaging and — let’s just say it — stunning digital products. Why? To meet our client’s objectives providing results and making the world a better place for their users.
  • - Product audit & consultancy
  • - UX research
  • - Concept design
  • - UX/ UI design
  • - Interaction and motion design
  • - UX writing
  • - Prototyping
  • - User validation
  • - Design systems
  • - Design consultancy
  • - Measurement strategy
  • - Data analysis
  • - CRO
  • - Content production

02.Digital Experiences.

It’s incredible how these two simple terms can contain a world of feelings. Digital experiences are a way of communication that goes beyond language. It’s about the many different feelings and interactions that you experience when faced with anything digital. It’s about creating a narrative that adds value. It’s about providing results to accomplish our client’s needs and make the user tick.

03.Digital-First Branding.

Have you ever smelt a perfume and automatically the receptors within your nose rush to your brain’s postbox and slip in an image of the person who wore it? Well, that’s branding — the faculty of being remembered in many different ways. We just happen to make those memories unique and memorable.
  • - Brand strategy
  • - Brand identity
  • - Digital brand environment
  • - Content strategy

04.Technology.

The base for a successful outcome is also applying the right technological strategy. At Plastic, we know about technology. But most importantly, we choose the right technology to achieve the expected results you want and, therefore, help you improve. Because if misused, technology is as useless as a chocolate fireguard.
